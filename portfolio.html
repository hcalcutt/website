Research Data Analysis

Evolution Within Class 0 Protostars

A comprehensive research project investigating the physical and chemical processes in low-mass star formation. This work examines the evolutionary markers within Class 0 protostars to build a coherent picture of early stellar development.

The research combines high-resolution spectroscopic data and interferometry with statistical analysis to identify patterns of evolution in these earliest stages of star formation.
